About:

Hello world! I am Armando Silva. I am an individual who walks to the beat of his own drum, sometimes double bass, but also a person who can know his role when he needs to. I pride myself in my pride. I consider myself to be a loyal, hard working individual.

I have decided to jump into this adventure of coding and web development. It is certainly something I have had an interest in since I have been introduced to the internet. Wondering how it works. It must've taken such work, such new thinking, a curiousity, a will, a drive and courage. Yeah, it had my attention. I am currently enrolled (enthralled) in UCSD Coding Bootcamp. My hopes are to open up new options that will allow me to live the life I would like for my family and I.

My hopes are to become a bit more productive, creative, appreciated for what I can add to a team. I believe I have a valuable skill set that can be very useful to any team I find myself on. I try to push to be the best and push others around me to realize they are just as qualified. Thank you for taking the time to look over this, it is appreciated.
