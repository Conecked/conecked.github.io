About Me

Hello world! I am Armando Silva. I am a proud Mexican American new father of two, yes two under 20mos, yes I may be a little crazy, yes I follow the creed happy wife happy life,yes this is a run on sentence. Now where was I?! Oh yes, I am an individual who walks to the beat of his own drum, sometimes double bass, but also a person who can know his role when he needs to. I pride myself in my pride. I consider myself a loyal, hard working "normal" individual. Do I believe there is such thing as normal? No I don't haha but I feel I am more similiar to others than we all want to think. More in the fact that I think we all have very similiar worries, hopes, insecurites and egos (definitely not blind to the much craziness around us).

I have decided to jump into this adventure of coding and web development. It is certainly something I have had an interest in since I have been introduced to the internet. Wondering how it works. It must've taken such work, such new thinking, a curiousity, a will, a drive and courage. Yeah, it had my attention. I am currently enrolled (enthralled) in UCSD Coding Bootcamp. My hopes are to open up new options that will allow me to live the life I would like for my family and I. My hopes are to become a bit more productive, creative, appreciated for what I can add to a team. I believe I have a valuable skill set that can be very useful to any team I find myself on. I try to push to be the best and push others around me to realize they are just as qualified.

I'm not quite sure where this journey is going to take me, but I'm certainly excited, anxious, nervous, hopeful and hoping I'm not being naive. I do feel my efforts will dictate what is to become, but that goes for every aspect of life. I truly want to make a better person of myself and hope this opportunity affords me the chance to seek that in a less stressful manner. Thank you for taking the time to look over this, it is appreciated. Here's to the good life!
